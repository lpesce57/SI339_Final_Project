Restaurants

No Thai

No Thai is a Thai restaurant that currently has 7 different location choices. It is very popular among University of Michigan students, inlcuding me. It is quick and easy but delicious! There is often enough for leftovers!

Best Menu Item:
Drunken Noodles with Beef
Pizza House

Pizza House is another favorite of University of Michigan students. It is located on Church Street and is close to both campus buildings and off campus housing. While, the name sounds like the only option is pizza, that is far from true. One of the most popular things to order is their feta bread.

Best Menu Item:
Feta Bread
Bricktops

Bricktops is located in Saint Louis, MO. It is a bit more fancy than the other two, but still just as delicious. It has typical American food and has a vast menu of appetizers, main courses and dessert options.

Best Menu Item:
Palm Beach Salad

Fast Food

Cane's

100% of survey participants voted Cane's as the #1 chicken restaurant in the world. The survey was only given to me, but regardless, Cane's won! Cane's offers chicken strips with their famous Cane's sauce. I have calculated the amount of sauces you will need to order per chicken strip and you will need one less sauce than you have chicken. The sauce can also be used to dip their spectacular fries in. Don't worry, I did not forget their Texas toast, that is just the right amount of buttery!

Best Menu Item:
Cane's Sauce
Chipotle

Chipotle allows customers to customize their dish with their variety of ingrediants. The menu options range from a quesadilla to a burrito, but the best option is their bowls. These bowls can be topped with rice, a choice of meat, beans, salsas, vegetables and more. Their chips and queso is the best way to top off a meal.

Best Menu Item:
Steak Bowl
McDonalds

A classic fast food restaurant that seems to be no more than a mile away from every location. They have an expansive list of choices with additions for holidays and special occasions. Their soda fountain drinks somehow make the soda itself taste better.

Best Menu Item:
Big Mac

Apps

Homescapes

Homescapes is a game very similar to Candy Crush. You beat levels that have various challenges and earn stars. Stars can be used to further build your home and choose new components for it. In addition to the regular features, there are frequent events to compete in that rotate through time. Additionally, players can join a team of other people to talk about the game, give each other lives or compete in compeitions together against other teams.

Spotify

Spotify is a very popular music app. Not only do they have a wide collection of songs, they also have other options, including podcasts and background noise. Users can create playlists, browse mood mixes, see the similarity between their music taste and their friends' and much more. At the end of each year, Spotify even gives users a recap of their year of listening.

Tik Tok

Tik Tok is a popular video sharing social media. Users can post videos, interact with their friends' videos and even be exposed to new content through the For You Page. Tik Tok's algorithm is scary good and has been customized to each user's preferences. This is what makes leaving the app so challenging.
